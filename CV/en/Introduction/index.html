Introduction

My career started in 2010. I obtained the position of a lawyer in a small company. I have been growing as a professional since that time. I changed a few companies until I stopped in state-owned enterprise, where I passed my way from a regular lawyer to the Head of the Legal Department.

As for me, if a lawyer wants to be good at the work he does, he needs to understand the whole scope of processes in the company, he works in.

In my practice I strive exploring and understanding company's business strategies and processes, company's structure and core values. I make my legal decisions based on this research, so I can be confident that I am acting in the best interest of the company and my decisions can benefit company's strategies and corporate reputation.

I have knowledge and skills for successful analyzing of business-algorithms and efficient communicating with different departments of business, starting with production up to executive management. With successful analysis of organization's structure and business processes I`m able to decrease legal risks and provide legal security in all stages of management decisions.

There are a lot of successful projects in my portfolio, realized in the companies with a difficult structure of management. Systems of paperwork, personnel accounting, anti-corruption control, compliance control, privacy policies etc. were developed and implemented by me in different stages of my professional career.

For now, I`m an executive professional in the legal area with a set of hard and soft skills, competent in solving all types of legal issues.

My goal is to find a job in IT sphere, with ability to work with a strong and ambitious team of professionals who love and respect their work.
